Established in the year 1994

We "DASGOLDEN" are the most experienced manufacturer of a wide range of Neon Signage, LED Signage, Chanellium Letter, Mini Acrylic Letter, Metal Letter, etc. Welcome to our neon sign world, where creativity meets demand in advertising. Our in-house production ensures client satisfaction. Embrace sustainability with us, as we power our signages with solar energy, reducing long-term electricity costs. Join the eco-friendly brilliance and illuminate your brand in this vibrant world. Neon lights, known for their distinctive appearance and vibrant colors, are not only efficient but also energy-saving. Chosen for decorative purposes and signage, they offer a unique glow with a longer lifespan. Neon tubes are a popular fixture in indoor and outdoor advertising and are highly in demand in advertising for Shopping Malls,Holding sign, Retail Shops, Jewellery Stores, Bars, Discotheques, Hotels, Restaurants, and High Rise Apartments.
